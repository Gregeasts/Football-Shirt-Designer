-----------------------------

Size Guidelines

Sizes are measured in inches

-----------------------------

01. Small: Fits chest sizes smaller than 36 inches, suitable for heights 5'5" and below.

02. Medium: Fits chest sizes 36-40 inches, suitable for heights 5'5" - 5'10".

03. Large: Fits chest sizes 40-44 inches, suitable for heights 5'10" - 6'1".

04. XL: Fits chest sizes 44+ inches, suitable for heights 6'1" and above.

-----------------------------

Choose the right size for the best fit!
